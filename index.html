The goal of vybesync is to make venues run so smoothly that operations are invisible and guests are present for every drop, every moment, every time Innerbloom hits 5:46 or you start crying when Collins sings the I’ll Cover You (Reprise).

Those moments can only happen because of the people who are the lifeblood of those venues, and they deserve to be honored with the same energetic reciprocity that they give every person who sets foot on a dance floor or buys a ticket to a matinee.

You cannot fake a synced room.
Guests and staff know when it’s authentic.

to be in the room where it happens.

Built by people who live and breathe this community.

vybesync makes live music, performance, and theatre ecosystems more effective and efficient.
Our profits also circulate back to the people and rooms that facilitate those experiences.

Our guests, artists, and community deserve all of our attention.
